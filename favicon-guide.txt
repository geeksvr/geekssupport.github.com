To create a favicon for your geekssupport.com website:

1. Create a 16x16 or 32x32 pixel image with your desired icon
2. Save it as favicon.ico in the root directory of your website
3. Or use an online favicon generator like favicon.io
4. Add the following line to your HTML head section:
   <link rel="icon" type="image/x-icon" href="/favicon.ico">

Alternatively, you can use a PNG format:
<link rel="icon" type="image/png" href="/favicon.png">
